<h4>Patents</h4>
<p>This page displays your Patent activities.  The number of entries within each issued and pending patent will be displayed in () on the tabs.<p>
<p>The content of this page can be sorted by clicking on any column title.</p>
<hr>
<p>To <strong>add</strong> an additional entry, click the “+ Add …” button in the upper right portion of the page.</p>
<p>To <strong>edit</strong> an existing entry, click the blue text in the row you would like to edit.</p>
<p>To <strong>delete</strong> an entry, click the X at the end of the row you would like to delete. Then confirm the deletion.</p>
<p>To <strong>print</strong> this section of the CV, click the “Print this section” button in the upper right portion of the page or select “Print Section” from the Print menu found in the menu bar.</p>
