<h4>Grants & Contracts</h4>
<p>This page displays your present, submitted and past grants and contracts.<p>
<p>The rows of this CV section may be moved up and down to set the print order in the CV. Click the Up and Down arrows, or mouse drag the entire row vertically.</p>
<p>The number of entries within each group will be displayed in () on the tabs.</p>
<hr>
<p>To <strong>add</strong> an additional entry, click the “+ Add …” button in the upper right portion of the page.</p>
<p>To <strong>edit</strong> an existing entry, click the blue text in the row you would like to edit.</p>
<p>To <strong>delete</strong> an entry, click the X at the end of the row you would like to delete. Then confirm the deletion.</p>
<p>To <strong>print</strong> a section of the CV, click the “Print this section” button in the upper right portion of the page or select “Print Section” from the Print menu found in the menu bar.</p>
